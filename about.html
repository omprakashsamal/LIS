Om Prakash Samal completed his master's degree in Library and Information Science from Khallikote University, Berhampur (which is now amalgamated with Berhampur University). During the programme he learned the library house keeping operations, knowledge organization (clssification & cataloguing), library automation software, digital library software along with reference management tools, e-learning and content management tools etc.

He is qualified for Assistant Professor in the UGC-NET conducted in December 2020 and June 2021 cycle. He also engaged in his dissertation project on Open Educational Resources (OER) and aspire to continue research ahead.
